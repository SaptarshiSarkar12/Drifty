## What is Drifty?

Drifty is a free, open-source file downloader that makes it easy to save content from the internet. Whether you need videos, music, or documents, Drifty provides a simple way to download them to your computer.

With both a graphical interface (GUI) and command-line interface (CLI), Drifty gives you flexibility in how you download files.

## Key Features

- **Free and Open-Source**: Drifty is completely free to use and will always remain open-source.
- **Cross-Platform**: Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux systems.
- **Fast and Reliable**: Downloads files quickly and handles interruptions gracefully.
- **Support for Popular Platforms**: Download content from:
  - YouTube videos and playlists
  - Instagram photos and videos
  - Spotify tracks and playlists
  - Direct file downloads from any website
  - Many other platforms and file types
- **Secure**: Downloads files directly without unnecessary third-party services.
- **Easy to Use**: Simple interface designed for users of all experience levels.
- **Multiple Interfaces**: Choose between GUI, CLI, or Docker depending on your needs.

## Why Drifty?

Downloading files shouldn't be complicated. Drifty gives you a straightforward way to save the content you need without dealing with:

- Intrusive advertisements
- Confusing interfaces
- Hidden fees or premium features
- Privacy concerns

Whether you're downloading a single file or automating batch downloads, Drifty provides the tools you need with both simple and advanced options.
